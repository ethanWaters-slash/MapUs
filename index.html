What is MapUs?

MapUs is a location-based photo scavenger hunt app that guides you through fun, creative challenges. Snap photos, track your path, and relive the journey—all in one app.

📸 Creative Prompts

Get random fun challenges like “Make a funny face” or “Find something blue.”

🗺️ Live Map Tracking

See your photos pop up on a map as you explore the world around you.

🧩 Visual Journey

Connect the dots between photo spots with a visual trail of your adventure.

📤 Share or Save

View your photo gallery and share your experience with friends and classmates.

Perfect For

  • Campus tours
  • Team-building events
  • Travel adventures
  • Icebreakers
